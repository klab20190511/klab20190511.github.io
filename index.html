Lodging


Our wedding and reception will be at the Majestic Yosemite Hotel (formerly called the Ahwahnee) in the Yosemite Valley. There are a variety of lodging options, depending on the experience you’re looking for:

  1. Yosemite Valley Lodge – centrally located in the Valley; 5-10 minute drive to the Majestic
  2. Majestic Yosemite Hotel - the site of the wedding
  3. Half Dome Village - cabins centrally located in the Valley; 5 minute drive to the Majestic
  4. Camping - options throughout the park
Lodging in Yosemite Valley books up quickly! We don’t have a room block, so we encourage you to reserve a room as soon as you decide you’re coming. All rooms become available to book starting one year + one day before the first day of your reservation.

Note: there are a lot of other lodging options outside the Valley, too. Be advised that these are around 45+ minutes away from the Valley.
